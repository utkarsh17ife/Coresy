About Us

With a vision to bring the best of the world to the masses, Core Systems Incorporated created Converged Reality – A patent pending platform that allows users to see augmented objects within a virtual environment.

This platform will allow users to see exhibits inside the Metropolitan Museum of Art while they are relaxing on the beach in the Bahamas, enjoying a virtual tour of New York City.

Core Systems Incorporated is a company that is focused on converging realities between the Virtual world and the Augmented world. Headquartered in Canada, the company boasts a development subsidiary in India that employs an extremely talented and experienced development team.

Our first product line is extremely versatile and spans three separate applications – Tourism, Social Media and Real Estate. We have painstakingly ensured the user friendliness of our products, to eliminate the need for highly technical skills, to deploy our products and make your sites CoReSy enabled.

Products & Services

The Magic Circle:

Our Hardware product that provides best in class, 3D position data in real time, including areas with minimal to zero cell network coverage. Critical placement of the 3rDi, allows users to capture their objects within range of The Magic Circle, assigning each object under that magic circle, a unique identifier associated with it.

3rDi:

The mobile app for Android and iOS for capturing and viewing objects and associating assets to the objects as well as the Web application to edit and upload the objects to the cloud. The 3rDi application allows users to take images, videos and 360 videos of objects, associate other image, audio, video and URL assets with the captured object and save the object. The mobile application is free of charge for the individual users while there is a nominal charge for the business user for the mobile application with the web backend application.

The Web application allows business users to edit the object, associate and edit the assets to the objects, add monetization tags and link to payment gateways and save the object with all its location metadata to the cloud.

An additional tab on the 3rDi mobile app allows users to recognize their position with respect to any deployed Magic Circles and view and download any object and its associated assets within range of that Magic Circle.
